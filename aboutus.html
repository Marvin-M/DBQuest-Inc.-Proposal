About Us

Year Established: 2004

WHO we are...

DBQuest, Inc. is a company committed to bringing cutting-edge yet cost-efficient solutions to technology requirements of small to medium enterprises (SMEs). It offers technical competence in Oracle and related software technologies, including multi-platforms and hardware. The company is an Oracle Certified Partner and an Oracle Authorized Education Provider. DBQuest is also a partner of DataOn Corporation and one of two SunFish resellers in the Philippines.



WHERE we came from...

DBQuest, Inc. was conceptualized by people who had spent years helping users of Oracle Technology. It was formed to assist companies to effectively use their database infrastructure investments, boosting their IT and end-users' productivity and increasing the efficiency and availability of the systems that these companies depend on daily.



WHAT we do. . .

Merging technologies is basically the company's apporach to the market today, both in the shirt-tern and long-term. DBQuest is not just a services company, nor a technology company but a strategic provider of cutting edge technologies that is bale to merge these into a complete solution to fit customer requirements and market demands. The company's strategic relationships with Oracle Corporation enable it to be in the position to provide best of breed products, expertise and best practice tot he customer and the market place in general.


At the core of its business philosophy are: its untiring and conscious effort to provide the best "solution" to customer and market requirements, a framework aligning business drivers and technology portfolios with a comprehensive understanding of market needs, and the drive to overcome business challenges through the effective use and efficient application of emerging technologies.



CORE COMPETENCIES

DBQuest's Senior Management Team has over 20 years experience of helping Oracle clients' DBAs, combined with 25 years of experience in using Oracle Technology and have accumulated a total of 10 Oracle Database Certifications. While working at Oracle Philippines, they received the Performance Award for Tier III, Education and Support Services for FY 2003 and FY 2004.
